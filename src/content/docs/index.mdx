## Overview

Gillytech is a full-stack web application for live, collaborative STEM
reasoning in CBC (Competency-Based Curriculum) classrooms. Students must
write their reasoning *before* answer options unlock, discuss with a small
group in real time, and submit — with all scoring computed and verified
server-side.

This site documents the requirements, architecturally significant
requirements (ASRs), architectural style, and architectural decision
records (ADRs) behind the system.
